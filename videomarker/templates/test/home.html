whether you call iT soccer, BASEBALL, HOCKEY or ANY OTHER SPORTS, we HELP YOU IMPROVE YOUR GAME. UPLOAD, MARK, SHARE YOUR PERFORMANCES.

 

Established in 2015, video marker is an application for all your video coaching needs. You upload your videos, mark the areas you want to highlight, share it with your friends and improve your performance. We're here to help you succeed.

 


    How it works


    Its simple

    • Register in our website
    • Upload your videos
    • Create tracks on your videos
    • You can create multiple tracks for the same video
    • Share your tracks with your students and friends

     

    Features


    Video guide provides 360 degree solution for you sports coaching needs. Video guide provide different set of features for different players in your team. Coaches can upload their game videos, edit them online, provide feed back to their students and students can even share their highlights with their friends. The tracks created by you will not be available for others.


    The videos can be uploaded, edited and viewed anywhere. Video guide is a completely cloud based solution. We also provide services to enable online video editing and marking your own video sites.
